A selection of projects I worked on

Your Mobility Footprint

Your Mobility Footprint is a student project at the TU Vienna. It aims to tackle the mental health issues caused by urban environments by suggesting people alternative paths through urban green spaces for their daily paths through the city.

In a design thinking process I gained hands-on experience in conducting literature reviews and expert interviews. For further idea generation I also designed, conducted and analysed a cultural probe. Furthermore I designed a design game and crafted the materials. The narrative poster on the left shows the whole ideation process of the project.

Bachelor Thesis

Design and implementation of a user interface for the data management of a collaborative task management system.


proCollab is a lightweight and life-cycle-based task management for Knowledge-intensive processes. The prototype already supports processes, task trees and tasks an the client side and data- management on the server side.


I created an UI-concept to support the data-management on the client side. It was designed to fit seamlessly into the existing UI. The implementation was realised using Angular, TypeScript and Sass
